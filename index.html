AVATAR

Avatar is an American epic science fiction film released in 2009, written, directed, co-produced, and co-edited by James Cameron. It premiered in U.S. theaters on December 18, 2009. It was one of the most expensive films ever made, with a production cost estimated at no less than $230 million. The film also set a record at the box office in the United States and Canada, earning approximately $278 million in its opening week. Within 10 weeks of release, Avatar crossed the $2 billion mark, becoming the hihest-grossing film in cinematic history, surpassing Cameron's previous film Titanic, which had held the top spot for 13 years. In contrast, Titanic took ten months to reach the same milestone.
